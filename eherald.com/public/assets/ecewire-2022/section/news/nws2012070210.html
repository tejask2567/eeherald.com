Amulet Technologies expands its European markets

Amulet Technologies has announced the continuation of its expansion into the European markets through new partnerships in the region:

1. LCDTEK, Inc to serve the United Kingdom
2. Adelco Electronics BV to serve the BENELUX area

Both distributors are specialized in displays and are therefore synergistic with Amulet products which facilitate customers to develop and run a graphical user interface.

Negotiations are in progress to finalize a partnership with a similar profile distributor in Italy, and the DACH territories continue to be covered by ACTRON AG, who became a regional distributor in 2011.

“We are experiencing increased demand of our solutions in the European markets, and these partnerships will allow us to provide local support and a regional presence” said Paul Indaco, VP of Sales and Marketing at Amulet Technologies. “We are very excited to welcome Adelco Electronics and LCDTEK as our new regional distributors in Europe”.

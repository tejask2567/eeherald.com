Erik Wood appointed as Ramtron's director of business development for F-RAM-based solutions

Ramtron International Corporation has appointed Erik Wood as director of business development, reporting to Ramtron's vice president of worldwide marketing, Scott Emley. Mr. Wood has 17 years of solution sales and high-tech market development experience as well as deep experience in the defense/aerospace, supply chain, transportation, oil and gas, healthcare, identification and security markets. At Ramtron, he is responsible for driving the development of strategic business opportunities for Ramtron's F-RAM-enabled semiconductor solutions.

"Erik's business development and executive leadership adds tremendous value to the ongoing expansion of our customer-facing engineering and marketing team," said Ramtron vice president of worldwide marketing, Scott Emley. "Erik's expertise in sales, marketing, and business development will help us identify and expand into key growth markets and cultivate high-growth customers that can benefit from the adoption of F-RAM-based products and solutions."

Mr. Wood had held positions of vice president of sales for Revere Security Corporation in Dallas, vice president of business development for Hi-G-Tek Inc., in Rockville and vice president and general manager of Symbol Technologies.

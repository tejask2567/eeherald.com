Apple opens an online store for Indian buyers

Digital device buyers in India can now buy Apple products from India specific Apple's online Store. Apple says its new online store to provide its customers the same premium experience found in Apple store locations around the world.

"We are proud to be expanding in India and want to do all we can to support our customers and their communities," said Deirdre O Brien, Apple's senior vice president of Retail + People. "We know our users are relying on technology to stay connected, engage in learning, and tap into their creativity, and by bringing the Apple Store online to India, we are offering our customers the very best of Apple at this important time."

The Apple India Store like any of its other store features online Apple Specialists to help with anything from custom-configuring any Mac to setting up new devices. Customers can get advice, receive guidance, and learn about new products directly from Apple, both in English and Hindi.

Other supports include financing options, available trade-in program, and Students can shop for a Mac or iPad with special pricing, and receive discounts on accessories and AppleCare+.

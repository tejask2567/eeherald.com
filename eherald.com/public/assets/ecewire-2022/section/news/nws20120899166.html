Zigbee software stack of Telit for M2M gets certification

Telit Wireless Solutions has announced the achievement of Zigbee PRO and Smart Energy software certification. Telit's in-house-developed software stack received certification for full compliance to standard version 1.1 for device classes Home Display and Energy Service Interface.

Telit explains ZigBee devices provide two-way communication and data transmission while not being subject to recurring air-time charges within the local environment as in the case of cellular wireless data.

"Telit'sability now to quickly customize its certified ZigBee PRO - Smart Energy stack means that our customers can integrate end products like utility meters and home control devices with surgical precision and fit to their market requirements", said Dominikus Hierl, chief marketing officer at Telit Wireless Solutions. "This makes Telit the first M2M vendor to meet one of the most fundamental requirements from system integrators. To provide them the ability to create products that can be easily adapted to various projects in different geographies thereby lowering their costs and improving ROI."

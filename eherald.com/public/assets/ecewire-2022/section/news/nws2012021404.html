Workshop on power-analysis attacks by Cryptography Research

Cryptography Research has said it is hosting a one-day workshop on power-analysis attacks and countermeasures on Monday, February 27, 2012 from 8:45 AM to 5:00 PM in our San Francisco office.

Introduction to side-channel analysis including simple and differential power analysis (SPA/DPA) is to be covered at Workshop. Live attacks going to be performed on mobile phones and the SASEBO FPGA platform using the DPA Workstation platform.

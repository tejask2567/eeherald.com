G.R.A.S. sells microphone kits for use with NI DSA hardware

National Instruments (NI) has announced that its alliance partner G.R.A.S. Sound & Vibration now sells microphone kits for use with NI dynamic signal acquisition (DSA) hardware.

NI offers microphone from array to condenser types appropriate for class 1 sound-level measurements. These microphones are used in applications like acoustic holography, environmental noise, noise mapping and sound power.

"For more than 10 years G.R.A.S. and NI have worked together on joint projects, and there has always been a great collaboration between our companies," said Peter Wulf-Andersen, managing director at G.R.A.S. Sound & Vibration. "I am proud that one of the biggest players in the industry has chosen G.R.A.S. as an official partner."

NI's new microphone kits include a G.R.A.S. microphone capsule and preamplifier. The microphones contain a transducer electronic data sheet (TEDS) chip and combine with NI DSA hardware to provide a plug-and-play sensor experience.

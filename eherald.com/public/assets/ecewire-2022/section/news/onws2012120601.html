Semiconductor tops the top-100 innovators by Thomson Reuters for 2012

Thomson Reuters has announced the top 100 innovators for 2012. Electronics industry is innovating more than other as per the list, semiconductors has even more presence. The semiconductor and electronic component companies in the list includes:

Processor experts: Advanced Micro Devices, Intel
FPGA makers: Altera, Xilinx
Analog Semiconductor IC experts: Analog Devices, STMicroelectronics, Texas Instruments
Corning; maker of components for LCD panels
LSIS; a green focused power electronics and power semiconductor developer,
LSI Corporation; Storage and communication IC developer
Marvel, Qualcomm in the wireless semiconductor area
Memory chip innovators: Micron, Samsung Electronics, SanDisk
Sharp; Multiple area expert
TDK; passive component maker
and TE Connectivity for interconnect solutions.

Telecom equipment companies in the list include:Alcatel-Lucent, Apple, AT&T,Avaya, Ericsson, Motorola, and NTT. Computer and accessories equipment makers in the list are Brother Industries, Canon, EMC Corporation, Fujitsu, Hewlett-Packard, Hitachi, IBM, NEC, Ricoh ,Seagate, Seiko Epson, Toshiba, Xerox.

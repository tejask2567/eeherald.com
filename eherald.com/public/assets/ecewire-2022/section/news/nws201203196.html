  Date: 19/03/2012

Cosmic Circuits demos its MIPI M-PHY at the MIPI Alliance event in Seoul

Cosmic Circuits has demonstrated its MIPI M-PHY solution at the recently held MIPI Alliance Demo Day in Seoul on the 13th of March. Cosmic Circuits M-PHY solution supports both the HS-G1 (1.5Gbps) and HS-G2 (3Gbps) modes and is available in multiple process technologies. Cosmic Circuits will be demonstrating their 40nm M-PHY's HS-G2 transmitter performance (eye diagram) using the Agilent 90K series oscilloscope and the HS-G2 receiver performance using Agilent's ParBERT and Bitifeye's Valiframe software. Engineers from Cosmic Circuits will also be available for discussions on Cosmic Circuits' MIPI solutions.

The Director of IP of Cosmic Circuits, Sundararajan Krishnan, commented that "As contributor members of the MIPI Alliance, we are very happy to participate in the MIPI Alliance Demo Day. Our MIPI D-PHY and M-PHY solutions are getting integrated into the SoCs of leading fabless companies around the world and given our strong roadmap that extends into the very advanced process nodes, we expect to see even more adoption. Application boards with our test-chip are available upon request to customers"

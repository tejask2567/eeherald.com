Cavium and PACE to demonstrate processor solutions for cloud and enterprise apps

Cavium, Inc. has announced that Cavium and its PACE partners to demonstrate solutions for secure cloud, enterprise, networking, data center, 3G/4G wireless infrastructure and network security equipment based upon Cavium's OCTEON, OCTEON Fusion, and NITROX processors, adapters and TurboDPI software at INTEROP 2012, Mandalay Bay convention center, Las Vegas, May 8 - 10th on booth 1927.

The products demonstrated includes:
1. Cavium's OCTEON Fusion processor line extends OCTEON's processing into fully integrated radio access devices with a combination of MIPS64 and multi-protocol baseband cores.

2. NITROX PX and NITROX III solutions for security applications run at up to 40Gbps/200K RSA Ops/sec in a single chip.

3. The NEURON Search processors provide L2-L4 network search for enterprise and service provider infrastructure equipment. These processors include support for both IPv4 and IPv6 rules and delivers 100 million to over 1 billion searches/second.

4. Cavium's HFA (Hyper Finite Automata) DPI hardware solution is available in OCTEON processors and can handle anti-virus, anti-malware, intrusion prevention, application recognition and URL filtering tasks.

5. The NITROX XL CN16XX-NFBE HSM delivers the FIPS 140-2 L3 performance at 45,000 RSA Ops/Sec and up to 5 Gb/s in bulk throughput.

6. The OCTEON family of Intelligent Network Adapters offloads secure network services and supports Gigabit Ethernet and 10 Gigabit Ethernet Network Interfaces.

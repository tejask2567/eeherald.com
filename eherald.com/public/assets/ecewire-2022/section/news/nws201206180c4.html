  Date: 18/06/2012

Mindspeed to display picocell tech at the 2012 Mobile Asia Expo in Shanghai, China

Mindspeed Technologies to display at the 2012 Mobile Asia Expo from June 20-22, 2012 at the Shanghai New International Expo Centre in Shanghai, China.

Planned demonstrations include:
A Transcede-based long-term evolution (LTE) small cell reference design with over-the-air (OTA) access to multiple commercial terminals;
A live demonstration of a time division synchronous code division multiple access (TD-SCDMA) femtocell, as part of China Mobile's commercial deployment (connected to the CMCC network); and
Highlights of Mindspeed's new T2200 and T3300 dual-mode small cell SoC reference designs.

Mindspeed is the leader in small cell technology, with 70% share of the high-speed packet access (HSPA) market according to data from ABI Research.

Mindspeed claims it is the only small cell SoC company that has demonstrated support for both HSPA and LTE in its small cell SoCs, and the only one to have shown both frequency-division duplexing (FDD) LTE and time-division LTE (TD-LTE) solutions.

Mindspeed is also participating in the Small Cell World Summit in London, June 26-28, 2012, where Dr. Doug Pulley, chief technology officer of the company's wireless business unit, to speak on the topic, "Small Cell Architecture for RAN Evolution," and will discuss data traffic models, deployment modeling, new strategies for network architecture, SoC architecture and the significance of dual-mode solutions during network buildouts.

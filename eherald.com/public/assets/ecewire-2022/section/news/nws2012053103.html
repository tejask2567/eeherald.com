Label manufacturer Nortec AMI partners with AdoptSMT in Europe

AdoptSMT Group Europe has announced the partnership for most of Europe with Nortec in order to provide their international clients with Nortec's standard and customized thermal transfer label solutions for applications across multiple markets. Nortec provides solution for total ID needs.

Nortec's Polyimide (high temperature) labels for automatic application fit with AdoptSMT's distribution and service of Hover-Davis label feeders and experience with pre-owned label applicators from manufacturer like CAB or Asys, Rommel or Nutek.

AdoptSMT offers the following Nortec products:

1. Masking labels - available for automatic application as well
2. Green Polyimide labels
3. Wire and cable labels
4. Die cut products

Doron Lavi, founder and president of Nortec said, "This is a major step forward in our abilities for continuing growth. Our team is extremely pleased with this European partnership and looks forward to the new possibilities that will result from this AdoptSMT cooperation, which already showed first impressive sales results at SMT Nuremberg two weeks ago".

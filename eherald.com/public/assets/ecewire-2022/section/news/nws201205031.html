Vigven Tech Mark is the new India representative of Pickering Interfaces

Pickering Interfaces has announced the appointment of Vigven Tech Mark Pvt. Ltd as their representative for India.

"We are very excited to be working with such a well known brand like Pickering," says V. Mahadevan, managing director at Vigven. "Our customers appreciate the fact that we are always looking for new brands to add to our product portfolio. Our innovative approach to selling combined with our line of varied and latest technology products makes us a considerable resource in Test and Measurement".

Bob Stasonis, Pickering's Asia/Pacific sales manager, added "Vigven's extensive knowledge of the Test and Measurement market, with a strong focus on modular test, combined with their complimentary product range, assures that our mutual customers have access to the right products for their application. We are very excited to work with the Vigven."

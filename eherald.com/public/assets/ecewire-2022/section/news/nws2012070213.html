Fairchild's GMRC facility at Shanghai offers advanced product testing

Fairchild Semiconductor has created the Global Mobile Resource Center (GMRC) that provides mobile technology designers the access to Fairchild's technical experts to provide compliance testing resources and diversified technology solutions.

The center is located in Shanghai, China and it offers a personalized customer experience with advanced capabilities for mobile device testing. Mobile designers now have a valuable option for their early-stage product designs. The GMRC engineering staff to conduct rigorous real-time testing for the design challenges facing high-speed signal, analog/digital audio and video, RF wireless communication, mobile power management and other mobile applications.

"Working closely with our customers to develop practical solutions is central to everything we do at Fairchild," said Roger Luo, senior technical manager at Fairchild. "With an advanced testing facility and a staff of industry leading engineers, the GMRC gives our customers a competitive advantage."

Testing offered by the GMRC includes:
1. Compliance testing of USB 2.0/3.0, HDMI/MHL, MIPI, and TDR testing
2. Sequential logic analysis, ESD and mobile power measurement
3. Analog/digital audio and video signal testing
4. Emulator testing for mobile RF devices, basebands and IP data traffic flows, RF power and spectrum testing

Fairchild's Global Mobile Resource Center provides immediate response and optimized solutions that can be instrumental in reducing the number of product returns.

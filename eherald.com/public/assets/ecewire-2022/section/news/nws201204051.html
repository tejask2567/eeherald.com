Honeywell awards its channel partners in India

Honeywell recently hosted its 4th edition of India region channel partners at the Honeywell Business Partner Conclave 2012 in Gangtok with a theme of 'Profitable Growth Together'. The event featured sessions on the company's strategic direction for the year, new initiatives, channel successes and keynotes by Honeywell Process Solutions (HPS) India executives. 19 channel partners were presented with 2011 HPS partner awards, including awards for partner of the year, outstanding performance, sales excellence, service excellence and special recognition.

This event offered HPS partners an opportunity to engage with the HPS' India leadership team and other partners on key issues such as the growth in process automation, global and regional strategies, best practices, operational and commercial priorities and channel management programs for 2012.

Honeywell's 2011 'Business Partner of the Year' award, which recognizes exemplary performance, was won by J. Systems and Services and Artee Flowwon Honeywell's award, for 'Outstanding Performance' for 2011, which recognizes exceptional performance, growth and customer service.

  Date: 29/06/2012

Sudhir Tangri is the new country manager for the EMG of Agilent Technologies India

Agilent Technologies, Inc. has appointed Mr. Sudhir Tangri as the new country manager for the Electronics Measurement Group (EMG) of Agilent Technologies India. Prior to his appointment to this position Mr. Sudhir Tangri was the vice- president & head of sales at Symphony Teleca Corporation.

Mr. Parmeet S. Ahuja, president and country general manager, Agilent Technologies India said, “I welcome Sudhir to his new role. We continue to build upon our leadership position in the Indian market and further strengthen our relationship with our customers. I’m looking to Sudhir's extensive industry experience combined with his result-oriented outlook to take Agilent Technologies India further on this successful journey.”

“I am excited to be back in the Test & Measurement industry, with the world’s premier measurement company. I will focus on further strengthening Agilent’s market share through aggressive growth of the India business by focusing on creating customer value and better customer experience” acknowledged Mr. Sudhir Tangri, country manager, Electronics Measurement Group, Agilent Technologies India.

Sudhir brings 2 decades of strong sales and marketing experience in various disciplines of Test & Measurement, Information Technology and Services, Telecommunications, Semiconductors, Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing etc. across leading companies such as Symphony Teleca Corporation, Aricent, Intel and Tektronix. Sudhir is a Bachelor in Electronics & Telecommunications Engineering, a Gold medalist from Gulbarga University.

RNCOS predicts 6% growth in global semiconductor market

According to RNCOS new research report, "Global Semiconductor Market Forecast to 2015", with the increase in future demand for electronics and requirements in new application areas, the semiconductor market is expected to sustain high growth momentum and anticipated to expand at a CAGR of around 6% during 2012-2015.

Some of the key findings of the report are:

- Integrated circuits dominate the global semiconductor market.
- Sales of Tablet, Smartphones, etc driving the global semiconductor market.
- Asia-Pacific will continue to dominate global semiconductor market.
- India Semiconductor Industry to witness tremendous growth.

Forte Design Systems to display its High-Level Synthesis at DAC

Forte Design Systems to demonstrate Cynthesizer SystemC high level synthesis (HLS) during the 49th Design Automation Conference (DAC) in booth #1430 in San Francisco, on June 4-6th.

The company to highlight Cynthesizer's advanced hierarchy support, custom transaction-level modeling (TLM) interfaces and other advanced features on an ARM based image processing system with control- and datapath-type blocks.

Phil Tharp, principal engineer at HighIP Design Company, will host a discussion on using Cynthesizer for the design of the HighIP USB models with a demonstration of the live hardware at 11 a.m. Wednesday, June 6, in Booth #1430.

Small Cell World Summit 2012: Octasic and Deltenna to showcase the Small cell

Octasic Inc has been invited to speak at the Small Cell World Summit 2012 in partnership with Deltenna Limited.

Andrew Fox, CEO at Deltenna, and Emmanuel Gresset, VP software defined radio from Octasic, to hold a session on how the latest system-on-chip (SoC) technologies have made it possible for OEMs to accomplish Micro cell performance within the physical size and power consumption of a Femto cell.

Deltenna and Octasic are to demonstrate the UMTS and LTE-ready Small cell – “the WhiteStar” at booth #3. The WhiteStar is a fully integrated, true SDR, multi-frequency and multi-standard, Small cell platform that can operate two standards in tandem through dual TRx 2x2 MIMO paths.

The Small Cell World Summit to be held in London, United Kingdom from June 26th to 28th 2012.

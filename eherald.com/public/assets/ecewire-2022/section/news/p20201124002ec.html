Indium forms strategic partnership with Valuetronics

Indium have formed a strategic partnership with Valuetronics International, a distributor of test and laboratory equipment markets to serve its materials to hand assembly and rework customers in the Americas with its cored wire, rework fluxes, and bar solder products.

"We are eager to work with Valuetronics to continue to deliver our high-quality products and technical expertise to our customers in the Americas," said Greg Hayes, Southeast Regional Manager and Americas Distributor Manager.

  Date: 18/06/2012

Wind River to display VxWorks running on Freescale T4240 at FTF 2012

Wind River has announced that it is a top-level Global Diamond sponsor of the Freescale Technology Forum (FTF) Americas 2012. Wind River is a long-standing Freescale partner and its presence is significant at the event which will take place June 18-21 in San Antonio, Texas. FTF provides attendees the opportunity to learn about the latest innovations in embedded design, engage with industry experts and attend in-depth technical sessions across a wide range of technologies.

Wind River’s participation to include technical sessions focused on embedded security, panel discussions on multi-core technology and virtualization, and a number of demonstrations that showcase the collaboration between Freescale and Wind River. These demonstrations will include Wind River’s VxWorks real-time operating system running on a simulated model of the Freescale T4240 via Wind River Simics, a full system simulator, in advance of hardware availability.

Wind River’s security presentations:
1. Improving security in Embedded systems: June 19th at 4 p.m.
2. Safe and secure partitioning platforms using Freescale technology: June 19th at 4 p.m.
3. Hardened Android for secure mobile communications: June 20th at 2 p.m.

Panel discussions:
1. Using software platforms to take advantage of the multi-core trend for ARM and Power Architecture Technologies: June 18th at 1 p.m.
2. Speed time to market with virtualization: June 18th at 3:15 p.m.

Wind River to demonstrate its software solutions for Freescale processors at booths 1420-1425 including:
1. Full system simulation with Wind River Simics
2. Powering Intelligent, connected health devices with i.MX processors
3. Wind River Android solutions
4. Wind River Linux for smart grid applications using Freescale platforms
5. Wind River development tools for Freescale QorIQ, i.MX, and automotive microcontrollers
6. Wind River Qt support on i.MX processors

For more information: http://www.windriver.com/events/ftf-2012/

DRAM market see a healthy 2% growth in third quarter of 2020

DRAM market remains healthy in third quarter of 2020 and also expected to see same level of growth in fourth quarter. Irrespective of the drop in prices of DRAMs, the demand is good in personal computer, mobile and graphics DRAM market. However server market remains weak due to excess inventory in that market.

Market researcher TrendForce reports the quarterly global DRAM revenue for 3Q20 increased by 2.0% QoQ to US$17.46 billion. If you look at the memory semiconductor' companies performance, Micron's DRAM sales revenue has seen a growth of 21.9% quarter over quarter in 3Q2020 with a market share of 25% in 3Q20. Taiwanese company Winbond’s revenue up by 11.3% QoQ, due to Huawei’s DRAM procurement.

Whereas other two leading suppliers Samsung's DRAM revenue for 3Q20 fell by 3.1% QoQ, and SK Hynix’s DRAM revenue for the same period also down by 4.4% QoQ, as per TrendForce.

Nanya Tech 's DRAM revenue for 3Q20 also down QoQ by 5.3%.

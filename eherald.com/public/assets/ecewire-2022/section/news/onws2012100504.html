Webinar on JESD204B serial interface’s benefits: Intersil to join Avago and Avnet

Intersil Corporation to join Avago Technologies and Avnet Memec in a webinar session created to explain designers the benefits of working with analog-to-digital converters that comply with the JESD204B high speed serial interface.

What: Free JESD204B serial interface webcast
Who: Ed Kohler, Strategic Marketing Manager at Intersil
When: Thursday, October 4th at 11:00 a.m. PT.
Where: To register: http://event.on24.com/r.htm?e=518652&s=1&k=B7196C72C6B19AABDF12C2D69899D560

Discussion outline:
1. JESD204 Basics
2. Description and Features
3. System Block Diagrams
4. Signals & Terminology
5. JESD204 Design Considerations
6. Specifications
7. Graphs
8. Calculations
9. JESD204 + FiberOptic Data Transmission Application Example

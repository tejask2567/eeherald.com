Posedge opens two new design centers in India and Taiwan

Posedge Inc has announced the expansion of its research & development centers (PRDC). The company opens two new design centers, each one in India and Taiwan.

The expansion in Taiwan and India will include an R&D center to serve local, regional and global customers. The new PRDC team in Taipei, Taiwan will host over ten RF engineers. The PRDC team in Bangalore, India will consist of over a dozen engineers laser focused on the growing wireless market segment with the development of Wi-Fi systems. This will include a clear focus on MAC and baseband technologies as well as DSP and algorithms.

"We are excited in the growth at Posedge and the opening of our new Taiwan and India sites," said Venkat Kodavati, VP, engineering, Posedge. "As part of our continuing commitment to the IP market, we have embarked upon a plan that leverages technology and innovation to better serve the needs of the global marketplace. To that end, Posedge is strengthening and expanding its research and development network; the expansion of the Posedge R&D centers will lead to breakthrough products and technology in the coming years."

Posedge recognizes the importance of establishing a solid intellectual IP base in the semiconductor market.

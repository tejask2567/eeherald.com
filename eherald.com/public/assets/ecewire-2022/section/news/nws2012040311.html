Rs.6499 tablet by Micromax is going to be a hit

The mobile phone maker Micromax knows the Indian user needs better than any other phone maker. Whether its long-battery lasting phones or recently launched android phones, all are hit in the market. That's the reason why Micromax has reached no.3 spot in Indian mobile phone market.

Now for the first time Micromax has announced Android 4.0.3 Ice Cream Sandwich powered 7 inch tablet called FunBook priced at Rs 6499. Micromax is relying on its education content providing partners Pearson and Everonn to add unique-value to its user.

Interesting part is, this devices is said to be produced at Micromax factory in Hardwar.

The key features and specifications:
Screen Size: 7 inch
Resolution: 800 x 480 pixels
Touch screen: Multi-touch capacitive touch interface
Processor: 1.22 GHz ARM Cortex powered processor chip and Mail 400 graphics processor core.
RAM: 512MB
Colors: Grey and Black
Memory: 4GB to expandable up to 32GB
Interface: HDMI, WiFi, 3G dongle
Camera: 0.3 MP front camera (not a good one)
Battery: 2.8 Ah (2800 mAh)

We predict this device going to be hit in the market. At a price of 6499, it has packed max compared to any other device. It's more made for the students with education content offering.

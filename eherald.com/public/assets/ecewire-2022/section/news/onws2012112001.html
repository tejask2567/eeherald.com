SiS touch controllers powers touch feature of Windows 8 systems

Silicon Integrated Systems says its SiS92xx touch controllers are adopted in both laptop and AIO PCs powered by Windows 8 OS.

SiS says its SiS92xx touch controllers conform Windows 8 rigorous touch specifications and provide the superiority of multi-point linearity accuracy, high response rate, high sensitivity, ghost point free, smart algorithm and high compatibility with various sensor patterns.

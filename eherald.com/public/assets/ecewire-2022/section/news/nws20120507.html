Dolby sound in KT-81 Karbonn mobiles for Indian consumers

Karbonn Mobiles has announced its collaboration with Dolby Laboratories to deliver pioneering Dolby sound to its mobile phones. The Karbonn KT81 marks the first from Karbonn Mobiles which incorporates cinematic Dolby sound.

Speaking on the collaboration with Dolby Laboratories, Shashin Devsare, executive director, Karbonn Mobiles says, "Constantly committed to bring to the mobile users of the country pioneering technologies to enrich their on-the-go experience, we at Karbonn Mobiles, intend to provide them technologies which help them derive the maximum utilization from their mobile devices. With this inherent thought process, we are happy to announce our association with Dolby Laboratories, which is the world leader in sound technologies. Through our association we intend to provide our consumers listening experience which is niche' yet affordable and brings to them top of the line music bouquet."

"Dolby changed the sound of movies forever, and Dolby Sound transforms the sound of mobile music," said Pankaj Kedia, country manager, Dolby Laboratories, India. "Dolby's collaboration with Karbonn Mobiles will help us enhance our ability to address the needs of mobile enthusiasts throughout the country. Now people can enjoy better listening experiences anytime, anywhere, whether using the speakers on their phone or a pair of headphones with Karbonn KT81."

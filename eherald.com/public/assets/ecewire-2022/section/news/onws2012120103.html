Webinar on medical electronics by ADI

Analog Devices, Inc. (ADI) to conduct a seminar on “Vital Signs Monitoring” for the Healthcare market. The Webinar will focus on Vital Signs Monitoring trends in the market and the technologies that Analog Devices and its customers are developing to address those trends. The presentation will include monitoring not only in the traditional hospital and clinical space but also will look at the emerging home, sports and fitness, automotive and consumer segments in terms of how these markets are looking to adopt and integrate Vital Signs Monitoring of patients and healthy individuals in to their environments.

Trends in the presentation would include ECG, Pulse Oximetry, Respiration and blood pressure monitoring, heart rate monitoring and as well as Glucose Monitors, Hearing Aids, Activity Monitors and Home Diagnostics along with the emerginguse of Smartphones and Tablets along with relevant Apps for health monitoring and diagnostics.

The Live Webinar will be presented on December 12, 2012 at 5 PM IST (Indian Standard Time).

The webinar presenter will be Tony Zarola, Strategic Marketing Manager in the Healthcare Segment at Analog Devices, Inc.

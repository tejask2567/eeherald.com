Evatronix partners with ICDREC for IP cores and software stacks

The silicon intellectual property (IP) provider Evatronix SA has signed a memorandum of understanding with the IC Design Research & Education Centre of the Vietnam National University HCMC (ICDREC). The memorandum establishes a partnership for IP cores and software stacks developed by Evatronix and ICDREC.

Evatronix will provide training and technology transfer to ICDREC to help lCDREC's engineers to be able to take advantage of Evatronix IP solutions, while ICDREC will provide Evatronix with outsourcing services that include hardware, software, IP core and SoC design and verification.

MATLAB EXPO 2012 concludes in Bangalore

MATLAB EXPO-2012 user event by MathWorks concluded recently in Bangalore was attended by 700 Indian design engineers from the Aerospace and Defence, Automotive, Computers, Electronics and Semiconductors (CES), Industrial Automation and Machinery, and Technology Services industries.

Customer presentations delivered at MATLAB EXPO echoed Dr. Prabhu’s keynote by demonstrating how many organizations throughout India are “Getting to Smart” by innovating with the help of MATLAB and Simulink. Examples presented included:

General Motors Technical Center India Private Limited: Utilization of Simulink Verification and Validation and Simulink Design Verifier for HVAC Controls Software

Infosys Limited: A Video Analytics Solution for Discovering Customer Shopping Behavior in Retail Stores

National Aerospace Laboratories: Desktop Real-Time Flight Simulator for Control Design

Honeywell Technology Solutions Lab: Modelling and Simulation of an Electro-Hydraulic Actuation System for a Variable Nozzle Turbine Turbocharger Using Physical Modelling Tools

Philips Healthcare - Software Competence Centre, Philips Electronics India Limited: Numerical Computational Modelling using Electrical Networks for Cerebral Arteriovenous Malformation

Goodrich Aerospace Services Private Limited: Implementation of Actuator Position Control in FPGAs

Moog India Technology Center: Automated Model-Based Requirement Coverage Analysis Tool

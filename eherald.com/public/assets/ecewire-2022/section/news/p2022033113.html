Lattice FPGA based online training courses by LEC2

World's number one exclusive FPGA semiconductor vendor Lattice Semiconductor announced the Lattice Education Competence Center (LEC2) to provide online training courses. Lattice says some of the topics of online courses are free from March 28 to March 31, covering Edge AI and vision application development using low power Lattice FPGAs, software tools, design techniques, and best practices. The course-series is sponsored by component distributor Arrow Electronics.

The topics include System Verilog for Synthesis and Simulation, Implementing PCI Express Designs with Lattice FPGAs, Device Architecture to Enable Vision Implementations, Plumerai's Efficient Edge AI Solution for Lattice CrossLink-NX, Lattice Propel Builder, TCL for Lattice Nexus Devices.

The link to online course is https://lec2-fpga.com/

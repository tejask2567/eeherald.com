Amino's A130M HD set-top box wins TMC 2009 IPTV Excellence Award

Amino has announced that its A130M HD set-top box wins Technology Marketing Corporation (TMC) 2009 IPTV Excellence Award presented by INTERNET TELEPHONY magazine.

The A130M is the sleek compact high definition (HD) STB measuring 11.4cm x 3.5cm.

"My congratulations to Amino for earning a place on our elite list of the 2009 IPTV Excellence Award winners," stated Rich Tehrani, CEO, TMC. "Their dedication towards improving IPTV solutions deserves recognition and I look forward to future innovations from them."

Amino Communications CEO Andrew Burke said: "As an independent specialist IPTV company, we take a great deal of care to bring products to the market that combine powerful performance and sleek good looks that consumers are happy to have in their homes. This latest recognition is very welcome ? and further reinforces our commitment to exceed customer expectations with our future products."

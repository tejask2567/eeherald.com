TriQuint announces winners of its 2011 Sales award program

TriQuint Semiconductor, Inc has announced the winners of its 2011 sales award program. The awards recognize TriQuint sales representatives’ and distributors’ overall contribution to TriQuint’s growth including technical support, increasing design wins, revenue and a high level of responsiveness to customers.

The members of TriQuint’s executive sales team nominated the award winners who were announced at TriQuint’s 2012 Global Sales Conference, an annual training and networking event for representatives and distributors.

TriQuint is focused on providing leading RF solutions to markets including fast growing smartphones and other mobile devices; base station, cable and optical networks, and radar applications.

The categories, winners and nominees are as follows:

Winner

Nominees

  Date: 13/05/2012

Marvell collaborate with LED lamp makers in providing reference design

Marvell has announced new light emitting diode (LED) lamp joint reference design in collaboration with LED lamp makers Cree and OSRAM.

The new A19 60-watt dimmable LED lamp reference design combines the Cree XLamp LEDs and Marvell 88EM8183 deep-dimming TRIAC LED driver integrated circuit (IC).

Other reference design is based on Osram's Brilliant Mix LEDs and the Marvell 88EM8801 LED driver IC
